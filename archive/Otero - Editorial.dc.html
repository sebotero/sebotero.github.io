About

I'm an Assistant Professor of Economics at Columbia University. I am also a Faculty Research Fellow at NBER and a Research Affiliate at IZA. I completed my PhD at Stanford and spent a year as a postdoctoral researcher at UC Berkeley.

I build models of how firms and consumers respond to policy — to understand what happens when governments change the rules. My work mostly focuses on education and labor markets.
